## Hi, I'm George 👋

I am interested in building **computational frameworks for complex biological and physical systems**, especially at the intersection of:

- 🧬 **Single-cell & spatial transcriptomics**
- 🧫 **Cancer evolution, tumor microenvironment, and multi-omics**
- 🤖 **Large language models, MoE, LoRA, and time-series foundation models**
- 🛰️ **GNSS meteorology, NTN, ISAC, and atmospheric sensing**
- 📊 **Scientific computing, data visualization, and reproducible research**

I like problems where biology, physics, statistics, and AI collide.

> From cells to space. From signals to mechanisms. From data to knowledge.
